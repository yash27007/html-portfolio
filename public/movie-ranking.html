  1. A Silent Voice

    A Silent Voice: A poignant symphony of empathy and redemption. We're drawn into the delicate dance of Shoya and Shoko, their past wounds resonating through tender animation and a powerful message of forgiveness. This melancholic masterpiece leaves a lasting echo of human connection and the transformative power of understanding.

  2. Weathering with you:

    Weathering with You: A breathtaking aria of youthful dreams and environmental anxieties. Makoto's sun-kissed journey intertwined with Hokuda's sacrifices resonates against the backdrop of Tokyo's tempestuous skies. This visually stunning spectacle blends fantasy and reality, leaving us pondering the weight of individual actions and the power of hope in the face of change.

  3. I want to eat your pancreas:

    I Want to Eat Your Pancreas: A bittersweet ballad of mortality and cherished moments. Sakura's illness casts a somber shadow, yet her poignant narration and Kakeru's gradual awakening to life's fragile beauty create a tender embrace. This melancholic gem teaches us to truly savor life's every fleeting moment and find meaning in deep connections, even those cut short.
